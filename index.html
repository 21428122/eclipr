Questions, answered

The stuff you're actually wondering.

Do I need followers to earn?+

No. That's the whole point. You post clips from your own account — the algorithm rewards a good clip, not a big account. Plenty of clippers earn with zero followers.

How do I actually get paid?+

Directly to your UPI — GPay, PhonePe or Paytm. Brands pre-load the campaign budget into escrow before it opens, so the money's already there. Hit the payout line and it's yours. No invoices, no waiting.

How is this different from YouTube/Instagram paying me?+

Those pay tiny ad-share (₹5–30 per 1,000 views in India). Eclipr pays from brand budgets — ₹50–150 per 1,000 — so the same clip earns you far more.

For brands: how do you stop bots?+

We verify real watch-time and engagement from the platforms' own APIs. Bought/botted views have none — so they're stripped before a rupee moves. You only ever pay for views that actually happened.

What does it cost to start?+

Clippers: ₹0 — free to join, earn from day one. Brands: you set your own budget and rate; there are no retainers or agency fees.
