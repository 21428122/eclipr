The model

A pool, a leaderboard, real reach

Instead of hiring one editor and hoping, you set a budget and open it to a whole network. Clips compete on performance, the best rise, and your spend flows to whatever actually gets watched.

  • You set the rules. Rate per 1k views, budget cap, do's and don'ts.
  • Clippers do the work. They source, cut, caption and post natively.
  • You approve & pay per view. Verified watch-time only — fraud earns ₹0.
