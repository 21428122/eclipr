Common questions

Pricing, answered

Do I pay upfront?

You fund a pool upfront, but that money is only spent as verified views come in. Unused budget stays yours — pause or withdraw anytime.

What counts as a verified view?

A view with real, engaged watch-time from a genuine account. We filter bots, view-farms and looped fraud before anyone gets paid — so you never fund fake numbers.

Who sets the CPM?

You do, within typical market ranges. A higher rate per 1,000 views attracts larger clippers and competitive niches; a lower rate stretches reach further.

Are there any retainers or platform fees?

No retainers and no lock-in. Eclipr takes a transparent margin on each campaign — you'll see exactly how your budget splits before you commit.

How do clippers get paid?

Instantly in UPI, once their views are verified. Fast payouts keep the best clippers active on your campaign.
