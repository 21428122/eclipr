The loop

Five steps, on repeat.

1

You send your content

A brand or creator funds a pool and drops in a video with simple rules — the rate per 1,000 views, the platforms, the do's and don'ts.

2

Creators clip it

Thousands of Indian clippers cut punchy shorts from your footage and post them to their own Reels, Shorts & TikTok. No footprint required — just a phone.

3

It distributes across every feed

Not one post — hundreds, natively, in parallel. Your content shows up where people already scroll, as content they share, not ads they skip.

4

Views get verified

We read real watch-time & engagement from platform APIs. Bots, farms and duplicates are stripped out before anyone is paid.

5

Everyone gets paid — in UPI

Clippers cash out instantly to UPI on verified views. Brands only ever pay for views that actually happened. Escrow keeps it honest for both.
