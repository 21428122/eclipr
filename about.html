We're building the fairest way for India's brands to buy attention and for its creators to get paid — where every rupee follows a real view, and the person who made the clip actually earns from it.

Why we exist

The internet runs on clips.
The clippers don't get paid.

Every day, thousands of young Indians cut clips that rack up millions of views — for free. Meanwhile brands burn budgets on reach they can't verify and influencer deals they can't measure. Both sides lose.

Eclipr closes that gap. Brands fund a pool; clippers compete to earn from it; and a verification layer makes sure money only moves on real, engaged watch-time. Fair to the creator, honest to the brand.
